Branchless Bank Logo


Creating logos has quickly become one of my favorite pasttimes. I love the flow of creative energy as I search my brain for relevant images for a company's brand.

The problem? A bank called Branchless bank needed an inviting logo.I started off sketching different associations that came to mind when I hear the word 'branchless'. Rather than chase the obvious tree branch route, I decided on a lotus flower. I opened up Adobe Illustrator and came up with an semi-abstract version of a lotus.

I quickly realized that my first draft wouldn't translate well. After all, could you even really tell what it was?I decided to make the logo pink and give it contour. Now, you could tell it was a lotus. An important rule for whether you've created a good logo is whether or not the image is scalable. Would this super detailed image work on a mobile screen? The answer is no. Back at it again.
Iteration was key throughout this process. I finally landed on a version that I loved and would work on any device.
