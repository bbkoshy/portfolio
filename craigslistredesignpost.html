Redesigning Craigslist


During the 2nd half of our cohort, our instructor tasked us with the challenge of redesigning the powerhouse site that is craigslist. Furthermore, he wanted the four students, including myself, to collaborate on this project as a group. We had one 10 days to draw up sketches, wireframes, and code.

Craigslist may not be easy on the eyes, but it consistently gets millions of page views a day. For our redesign, our group decided to keep the redesign simple. We didn't want to stray too far from what users are currently comfortable with.

We were each tasked with a page to redesign staying consistent within our brand guidelines. Sketches became wireframes which turned into coded and responsive sites. The site I was personally responsible for was the item listing portion. My end goal was visual hierarchy, responsiveness, and appropriate prioritization of the location along with the visuals. You can view the live coded version on github
