Sports League App


The challenge: Create a sports league app for recreational teams.The goal was to create an app where local rec league athletes would be able to log in and have access to team stats, schedule, and messaging.

This project was a lot of fun to create. Arriving at a product that was user friendly and fit the needs of a recreational athlete meant doing research into their needs and subsequently creating user personas to guide the flow and intention of the app.

I created this app as mockups in photoshop and uploaded them to invision where they were prototyped. Check it out!
